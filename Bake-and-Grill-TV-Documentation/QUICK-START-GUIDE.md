# ⚡ Quick Start Guide - Bake and Grill TV

## For Your Friends to Test! 🧪

This is a quick reference guide for testing the Bake and Grill TV platform.

---

## 🚀 Login

1. Go to: **https://tv.bakeandgrill.mv**
2. Login with:
   - **Phone Number** (7 digits) OR **Email**
   - **Password**

---

## 📺 Watch TV (5 Steps)

1. Click on a **Playlist** card
2. Browse or **search** for a channel
3. **Click** the channel to start watching
4. Use **controls** to pause, adjust volume, fullscreen
5. Click **channel list** to switch channels

**That's it!** 🎉

---

## 📱 Display Setup (For TVs)

### Quick Setup with QR Code:

1. **On TV**: Open browser → Go to `tv.bakeandgrill.mv/#/pair` → Click **"QR Code"** tab
2. **On Phone**: Login → Display Management → Pair New Display → **Scan QR code** from TV
3. **Fill Details**: Name, Location, Playlist
4. **Done!** TV starts playing automatically ✨

### Alternative - PIN Code:

1. **On TV**: See 6-digit PIN on pairing page
2. **On Admin**: Display Management → Pair New Display → Enter PIN + details
3. **Done!** TV connects and starts playing

---

## 🎮 Remote Control

1. Go to **Display Management**
2. Click **"🎮 Remote Control"** on an online display
3. **Search** or **filter** channels
4. **Tap a channel** → Switches instantly! ⚡
5. Use **volume controls** as needed
